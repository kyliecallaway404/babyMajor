Major's Birth Story


I was due on November 19th the Monday before Thanksgiving. I went into the doctors office and had not progress. The doctor recommended I schedule an induction. They were supposed to call me in the next few days. They did not and were closed on Thursday and Friday. I finally called on Monday the day after I was supposed to be incude! I was frustrated! They were able to get me in the following morning. Major was born just after midnight that night.
